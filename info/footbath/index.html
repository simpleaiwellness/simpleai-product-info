Mugwort Footbath Pack

Gentle daily wellness ritual
1 sachet (30g) 43–45°C 20–25 minutes

Directions

  1. Place 1 sachet into a foot tub.
  2. Add hot water and steep for 3–5 minutes.
  3. Adjust temperature to 43–45°C.
  4. Soak feet for 20–25 minutes.

Caution

  • For external use only.
  • Not recommended during pregnancy.
  • Do not use on broken or infected skin.
  • Stop use if irritation occurs.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
  • Not a substitute for medicines or medical devices.

Ingredients

Mugwort, Safflower, Sappan Wood, Vaccaria Seed, Glossy Privet Fruit, Cinnamon Twig, Mulberry Twig, Motherwort, Sargentodoxa Vine, Selaginella Herb, Tangerine Peel, Peony Flower.
