Mugwort Footbath Pack

1 sachet (30g) 43–45°C 20–25 minutes
This page provides directions, cautions, ingredients, and manufacturer information for the product.

Directions for Use

  1. Place 1 sachet into a foot tub.
  2. Add hot water and steep 3–5 minutes to release the herbs.
  3. Adjust temperature to 43–45°C.
  4. Soak feet for 20–25 minutes.
Tip: Add water gradually and test temperature before soaking.

Caution

  • For external use only. Do not ingest.
  • Not recommended during pregnancy.
  • Do not use on broken, swollen, or infected skin.
  • Discontinue use if discomfort, redness, itching, or burning occurs.
  • Keep out of reach of children.
  • This product is not a substitute for medicines or medical devices.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place. Avoid direct sunlight. Keep sealed after opening.

Ingredients

Mugwort, Safflower, Sappan Wood, Vaccaria Seed, Glossy Privet Fruit, Cinnamon Twig, Mulberry Twig, Motherwort, Sargentodoxa Vine, Selaginella Herb, Tangerine Peel, Peony Flower.
