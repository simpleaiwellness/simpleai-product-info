智能艾灸舱

Usage Instructions & Safety Information

使用说明及安全信息

How to Use

使用方法

  1. 1

    Remove all metal jewelry and accessories before entering the chamber.

    进入艾灸舱前，请取下所有金属首饰和配饰。

  2. 2

    Change into the provided comfortable clothing or disposable gown.

    换上提供的舒适衣物或一次性长袍。

  3. 3

    Lie down comfortably in the chamber. Our staff will adjust settings for you.

    舒适地躺在舱内，我们的工作人员会为您调整设置。

  4. 4

    Session duration: 30–45 minutes. You may feel gentle warmth.

    疗程时间：30–45分钟。您可能会感到温和的热感。

  5. 5

    Drink warm water after your session. Avoid cold drinks for 2 hours.

    疗程后请喝温水，2小时内避免冷饮。

Cautions

注意事项

  • Do not use if you have a fever or acute inflammation
  • 发烧或急性炎症期间请勿使用
  • Avoid use within 1 hour after meals
  • 饭后1小时内避免使用
  • Stay hydrated — drink water before and after sessions
  • 保持水分 — 疗程前后请多喝水
  • If you feel dizzy or uncomfortable, alert staff immediately
  • 如感到头晕或不适，请立即告知工作人员
  • Avoid showering immediately after; wait at least 4 hours
  • 疗程后请勿立即洗澡，至少等待4小时

Do NOT Use If

禁忌人群

  • Pregnant or breastfeeding
  • 孕妇或哺乳期妇女
  • You have a pacemaker or implanted medical device
  • 装有心脏起搏器或植入式医疗设备
  • You have severe heart disease or high blood pressure
  • 严重心脏病或高血压患者
  • You have open wounds or skin infections
  • 有开放性伤口或皮肤感染
  • You are under the influence of alcohol
  • 饮酒后
  • Children under 12 years without adult supervision
  • 12岁以下儿童需成人陪同
